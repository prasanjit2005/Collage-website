INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Biswanath College

Biswanath College, established in 1960, is one of the premier institutions of higher education in Assam, affiliated to Gauhati University. The college has been a center of learning for students across diverse backgrounds, imparting quality education and promoting research, innovation, and cultural development. With a mission to nurture responsible citizens and professionals, the institution emphasizes academic excellence, value-based learning, and community engagement.
